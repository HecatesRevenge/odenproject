Chicken Parmesan

Description

Indulge your taste buds in the culinary delight of our extraordinary Chicken Parmesan recipe. Succulent chicken breasts, perfectly seasoned and coated in a crispy golden breadcrumb crust, are smothered in rich marinara sauce and topped with a lavish blend of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. Baked to perfection, this dish offers a harmonious symphony of flavors and textures that will transport you to Italian gastronomic bliss. Each savory bite delivers a tantalizing combination of tender chicken, zesty tomato sauce, and the creamy decadence of melted cheeses, making this Chicken Parmesan a truly unforgettable dining experience. Elevate your home-cooked meals with this unparalleled recipe that captures the essence of comfort and sophistication on a plate.

Marinara Sauce

  • 3 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 (28-oz.) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 large sprigs basil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Chicken

  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp.garlic powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups finely grated Parmesan, divided
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 6 (1/4"-thick) boneless, skinless chicken cutlets (about 1 1/2 lb. total)
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup vegetable or canola oil
  • Chopped fresh basil, for serving
